Hello, I am Nico. Biologist interested in mechanisms governing cognitive control. 
I like to ride my bike, eat veggies and listen to musics. 



## Background

Grew up in the Rockies, avid runner/skiier, hoped to conquer asthma through altitude training (didn't work). 
Had a brief stint in stint in the Netherlands living near the border of Germany. There I gained an appreciation for genetics and cycling. Subsequently spent some years in Argentina, one in rural Cordoba and another in the capital Buenos Aires. There I gained an appreciation for Yerba Mate and biology. Moving back stateside, I finished Highschool in Houston and set off to Baylor for undergraduate studies in Biology. Thereafter I spent sometime working at the University of Texas at Austin before moving to Bethesda Maryland to work at the National Institues of Health.
